Suggested Usage:
-Prep: Using your fingers or a brush, apply a dime-size amount of HD Microperfecting Primer all over the face before foundation or simply wear alone.
-Cover: Apply one to two pumps of HD Invisible Cover Foundation to the center of the face and blend outward with your fingers, a brush, or sponge.
-Finish: Apply a small amount of HD Microfinish Powder all over the face in a circular motion using HD Kabuki Brush.
