Suggested Usage: 
-To prime: Apply to clean skin using the included sponge or a complexion brush. Follow with your favorite Stila beauty balm or foundation. 
-To set: Swirl sponge in product and apply over makeup using a tapping motion, being careful not to sweep or drag sponge over makeup. 
- To mattify: Use sponge to tap over the areas of the face, such as the t-zone, to absorb excess oil and eliminate shine.
