Suggested Usage:
-Apply after using your Redness Solutions Redness Regimen or 3-Step Skin Care.
-Shake bottle well. Squeeze a few drops of foundation onto the back of your clean hand.
-With Foundation Brush or clean fingertips, smooth makeup onto skin, starting in the center of the face, blending out toward hairline and jawline.
-Remove with your favorite Clinique oil-free makeup remover.
