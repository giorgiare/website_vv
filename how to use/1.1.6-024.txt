Suggested Usage:
-Swirl a small amount of Faux Tan into the lid, using the Angled Face Brush, until all the Faux Tan has been tucked into the bristles.
-Tap away any excess. There should be no visible Faux Tan on the outside of the brush.
-Buff onto areas sun would naturally hit your face such as forehead, eyes, cheeks and bridge of nose.
-Apply on top of bareMinerals SPF 15 Foundation.
