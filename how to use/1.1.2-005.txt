Suggested Usage:
-Shake well before using. 
-Extend arm straight out and bend at elbow 90 degrees. 
-Close your eyes. 
-In one quick, continuous motion, spray a layer of foundation over the face in the shape of a Z, starting with the forehead and ending on the chin. 
-Use a headband or tissues to protect clothing as needed. 
-Or, spray foundation onto a kabuki brush and buff into face for fuller coverage.
