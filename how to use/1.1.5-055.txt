Suggested Usage:
-Twist the base and dispense the product onto the back of the hand.
-Then, use the brush to apply to the center of the face, sweeping outward, in upward movements.
-Gently tap with the fingers to blend into the skin.
-It can be used alone to add radiance to the forehead, nose chin and cheekbones, or over the entire face as a base under foundation to make application smoother and extend its wear.
