Suggested Usage:
-To apply Secret Camouflage: Pick up product with Secret Camouflage Brush. Use feather-like strokes to the darkened areas (blemishes, shadows, scars).
-To apply Secret Concealer: Pick up product using the Secret Camouflage Brush and apply to brighten and minimize dark shadows around the eye area only. Wipe the brush clean after use. Shake a small amount of Translucent Loose Setting Powder into the lid. Pick up product with Velour Puff and gently press and roll the Velour Puff across the face, working the powder into the skin.
