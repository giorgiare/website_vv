Suggested Usage:
-Apply on bare skin or over foundation.
-Touch on with wand where skin needs line-softening cover. Or, finger-blend into skin using a light, patting motion, or use Concealer Brush.
-To reduce look of facial lines and wrinkles, apply along length of line; smooth across grain of line with Concealer Brush or fingertip. Repeat for more coverage.
-Avoid rubbing once application is complete.
