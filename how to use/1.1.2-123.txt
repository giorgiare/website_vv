Precautions: 
-Close the inner lid of compact refill securely after each use and store away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures to ensure the integrity of this formula. 
-Always keep sponge clean for a smooth, even application.

To insert refill foundation: 
-Insert the closed refill into the compact base. 
-To assure correct positioning, the hinge should be positioned to the left. 
-Press down on refill until it snaps into place.
