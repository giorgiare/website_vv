Suggested Usage:
-Apply after using your 3-Step Skin Care System.
-Start in center of face and blend outward with Foundation Brush or fingertips.
-Blend evenly for smooth, natural-looking coverage. For a seamless look, dampen fingertips and blend edges, especially along jaw line.
-To camouflage discoloration, build layers of Continuous Coverage SPF 15 and loose powder.
-Remove with your favorite Clinique makeup remover.
