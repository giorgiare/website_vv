Suggested Usage:
-Dot the product over the entire face, avoiding the eye area.
-Start with the cheekbones and blend outward, then smooth downward on the lower part of the face.
-Finish with the forehead.
-Blend the excess product into the skin with fingertips if necessary.
-After application, enjoy the sensation of natural, invigorated freshness?the best proof of the formula's efficiency.
-Wait two minutes for the full effect, and then apply your makeup.
