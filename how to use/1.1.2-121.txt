Suggested Usage:
-Squeeze 1-2 pearl sized drops of foundation and apply with a patting/stippling motion pressing in the foundation with fingers, brush or sponge. 
-Make sure all angles of lines, pores and uneven skin surfaces are covered. 
-Sheer to moderate coverage is created with layers. 
-Set with powder.
-For best results prep skin prior to makeup. 
-Use Maifanshi Makeup Color Base as primer before foundation.
