Suggested Usage: 
-Shake before use. 
-Apply a small amount using fingertips or a brush starting at the t-zone and working out to the hair and jawlines, being careful to blend in. 
-Use finger to tap more product to areas that may need a little extra coverage. 
-For an allover glow, mix half a pump of Becca Shimmering Skin Perfector with Radiant Skin Satin Finish Foundation and apply to the entire face starting with the t-zone.
