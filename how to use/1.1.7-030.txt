Suggested Usage:
-To soften fine lines around the mouth area. 
-Apply dabs of concealer to the corners of the mouth, then blend in and smooth. 
-To open the eyesApply a dab of concealer to the inner corner of eyes, then blend in to brighten the eye area. 
-Apply small dabs under the eye, around the nose, along the brow bone, and below the mouth. 
-Blend in and smooth product with fingertips or with Professionnel Concealer brush #46. 
-Apply your usual foundation.
