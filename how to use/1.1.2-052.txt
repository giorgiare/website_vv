Suggested Usage:
-Apply a small amount with the Cream Foundation Brush, starting in the center of face blending outward until the desired coverage is achieved.
-Build Total Cover Cream Foundation on areas/spots that require more coverage.
-Set with Matte or Illuminating Setting Powder.

Precautions:
-The sun may cause sunburn, premature aging of the skin, and skin cancer.
-Avoiding the sun, wearing protective clothing, and regular use of sunscreens over the years may reduce the chance of these harmful effects.
