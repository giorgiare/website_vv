Suggested Usage: 
Airflash CC Primer is a universal product that works best when applied before any other complexion makeup on clean, moisturized skin. Use under foundation to set and hold, or alone for a radiant glow. 

-Shake well before use. 
-To apply directly onto the face, hold the bottle 18 inches away. 
-Close your eyes and spray in a single smooth “Z” motion for even coverage. 
-If desired, smooth product with your hands to blend. 
-To apply with your hands, spray the product onto the palm of your hand and tap gently onto the face. 

Airflash Regimen: 
-Prime with Airflash CC Primer. 
-Perfect with Airflash Spray Foundation. 
-Finish with Airflash Matte Touch.
