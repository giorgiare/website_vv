Suggested Usage: 
-Shake well prior to each application. 
-Apply evenly to face. 
-Blend outward using fingertips for uniform coverage. 
-Can be worn alone or with makeup. 

Precautions: 
-If pregnant or breast-feeding, consult your physician before use. 
-Do not stay too long in the sun, even while using a sunscreen product. Over-exposure to the sun is a serious health threat. 
-Apply the sunscreen product before exposure. Re-apply frequently to maintain protection, especially after perspiring, swimming or toweling.
