Suggested Usage:
When using with Sephora Collection Bronzing Powder Brush #43: 
-Pick up powder. 
-Brush powder on prominent features: cheeks, forehead and nose. 
-Blend using circular movements for a natural, light tan. 

When using Sephora Collection Relief Powder Brush #44: 
-Pick up powder, hold brush upright and tap the base to remove excess powder. 
-Brush over all face from center to outer edges using circular movements, focusing on the prominent features of the face: cheeks forehead and nose.
