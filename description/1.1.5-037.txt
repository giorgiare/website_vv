What it is:
A creamy, smoothing primer for long-lasting makeup wear that minimizes the appearance of pores and fine lines.

What it does:
Formulated for all skin types, this creamy primer "resurfaces" skin and creates a fresh, smooth canvas for optimal makeup application that lasts all day. Special polymers act as fillers, visibly smoothing the look of pores and fine lines, while glycerin helps keep skin hydrated.

What else you need to know:
This formula is free of parabens, alcohol, and fragrance.
