What it is:
A natural-looking mineral powder foundation.

What it does:
AmazingCosmetics Velvet Mineral Powder Foundation is a silky-soft good-for-your-skin mineral loose powder foundation that glides on easily, allowing your skin to breathe while creating a naturally flawless finish, with an even tone and texture. It's formulated to minimize the appearance of pores and fine lines while creating a matte finish for the face.

What else you need to know:
You can completely control the coverage you desire with a swipe of your brush. There is no reason to work the minerals into the skin, just gently stroke Velvet Mineral Powder Foundation onto the face as you would when applying blush. It's that simple.
