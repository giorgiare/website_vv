What it is:
A long-wearing blush. 

What it does:
Inspired by the bestselling Halo powder, this blush gives a luminous, lit-from-within flush that lasts. As an added bonus, the innovative built-in grinder gives you total control to get the perfect amount of freshly ground, beautiful color every time—and with no mess. For the ideal amount of product, try twisting the grinder clockwise from noon to 1 o’clock.
