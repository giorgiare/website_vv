What it is:
An opaque, camouflage liquid makeup that goes on natural and covers flaws, birthmarks, and scars.

What it does:
Get lasting camouflage for every skin type. This opaque makeup covers flaws, birthmarks, and scars and can be used as a concealer or as full-face makeup. It provides a physical sunscreen (with titanium dioxide) and delivers a natural matte finish.

What else you need to know:
It works wells with dry to dry-combination skin and oily-combination or oily skin types for special occasions.
