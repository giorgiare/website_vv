What it is:
A three-in-one compact powder. 

What it does:
Consider this a makeup survival kit for all skin types. This compact can be used as a wet or dry foundation or as a finishing powder. For a soft matte effect, apply over daytime moisturizer. Sponge product on damp for a full-coverage, flawless matte finish. As a powder: puff on dry over any Lancôme foundation.
