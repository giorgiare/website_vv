What it is:
An ultra-fine light beige powder with luster and SPF 20 protection that easily blends into the natural skintone. 

What it does:
This combination of two types of finely milled and cut loose powders provides even, silky coverage and reflects light to give skin a flawless finish with radiance. It protects skin from drying using jojoba seed oil combined with silk powder and sodium hyaluronate, two high-performing moisturizing ingredients. The formula also shields skin from harmful UVA and UVB rays.

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
- Synthetic Fragrances
- Synthetic Dyes
- Phthalates
- GMOs
- Triclosan
