What it is:
An ultra-fine pearl powder formula that covers pores, balances your skin, and sets the foundation.

What it does:
Maifanshi Natural Lighting Powder uses a unique, ultra-fine pearl powder formula to camouflage uneven skintones. The soft, refined pearls diffuse light to cover pores and balance skin surfaces for a natural, luminescent glow. 

What it is formulated WITHOUT:

- Sulfates
- Synthetic Fragrances
- Synthetic Dyes
- Phthalates
- GMOs

What else you need to know:
Moisture-retaining hyaluronate in the powder makes it ideal for dry skin. This product can be worn by itself for a nice soft glow or as a finishing powder to set your foundation.
