What it is:
A stick concealer that reduces the appearance of dark circles using color correcting technology. 

What it does:
With a sheer finish, this corrector neutralizes dark circles with exclusive color and an ultrathin formula specifically for the eye zone. The unique formula not only cancels the discoloration around the eye based on the skintone around the cheek but it also boosts the skin’s radiance and smooths fine lines, helping to improve the skin’s texture.
