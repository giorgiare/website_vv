What it is:
A dermatologist-formulated color correction cream combining seamless coverage, antiaging benefits, and SPF protection in a breathable, natural-looking formula. 

What it is formulated to do:
Created by Dr. Gross for patients to achieve a beautiful, even complexion after dermatological treatments, this multitasking potion goes a step beyond BB creams. Available in four shades to suit all skin types, it contains powerful antioxidants vitamins C and E to prevent collagen breakdown, avobenzone and octinoxate to keep skin protected from sun damage, and hyaluronic acid to keep skin moisturized and refreshed. 

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Sulfates 
- Phthalates
