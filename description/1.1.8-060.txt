What it is:
A creamy, silky, sheer powder blush that delivers a pop of color to cheeks. 

What it does:
True Hue™ Blush contains rhodiola, an arctic plant root that makes cheeks appear refreshed, allowing them to come alive with color. Whether you’re looking for a luminous flush or a touch of vibrance, this buildable, featherweight formula allows you to create your true hue. 

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates 
- Phthalates
