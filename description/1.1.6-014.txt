What it is:
A blended powder with a loose, lightweight texture that's great for all skin types.

What it does:
Clinique's signature blended powder is right for every type of skin. It sets and perfects makeup, makes pores seem to disappear, and the airy, skin-hugging formula delivers a reliably flawless finish. It has a sheer, super-fine texture that smoothes the skin's appearance, leaving a natural, matte finish. It is mineral enriched and absorbs surface oil.
