What it is:
A micropigmented pressed powder.

What it is formulated to do:
Eliminates unwanted shine, evens the skin, and sets foundation for all-day wear. Antiaging ingredients help keep skin healthy.

What else you need to know:
Micronized pigments "float" on the skin to even out skin tone. An exclusive combination of complexion perfectors including seaweed extract, chamomile, vitamins A, C, and E, as well as soothing aloe vera help to increase elasticity, moisture, and firmness.
