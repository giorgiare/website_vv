What it is:
A quad of matte bronzing powder tones. 

What it does:
This perfect selection of graduated matte tones creates a long-lasting, natural look of radiance at any time of year. This sunkissed palette is perfect for sculpting and highlighting: use the pink or dark shades for your blush and the more neutral or nude shade to unify the rest of your face. Each woman can choose and blend shades to create her ideal color. 

What else you need to know:
To enhance the 4 Seasons experience, Guerlain has added four new harmonies of coordinated shades to better accommodate women’s varying complexions. With a total of six shades to boast, Guerlain ensures that all women, fair or dark, can indulge in the pleasure of a Terracotta glow.
