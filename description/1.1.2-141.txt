What it is:
An oil-free, long-lasting foundation that responds to skin's specific needs. 

What it does:
This foundation increases moisture in dry areas and minimizes shine for a semi-matte finish and medium coverage. Formulated with an exclusive ingredient complex of hyaluronic acid and hydro-wrap vitalizing DE complex, this product promotes ideal moisture levels in skin's dry areas. It also contains prismatic nano-powder to increase luminosity and conceal pores.
