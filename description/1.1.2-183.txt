What it is:
A light liquid foundation that melts into the skin with natural-looking coverage.

What it does:
ELLIS FAAS believes that foundation should illuminate the skin without feeling or appearing like a mask. With an exceptional balance of high-quality ingredients, Skin Veil glides on in a thin layer, blending into the skin. Skin Veil minerals diffuse light across the skin’s surface, reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. ELLIS FAAS foundation incorporates moisturizing ingredients, which nourish the skin, as well as up to SPF 15 PA+++ for ultimate sun protection.

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Synthetic Fragrances
- Triclosan

What else you need to know:
This product is recommended for all skin types, including sensitive skin.
