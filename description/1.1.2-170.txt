What it is:
A long-wearing foundation with an advanced formula that binds with the skin for flawless, comfortable, 16-hour coverage. 

What it does:
Guerlain’s first-ever Tenue De Perfection time-proof foundation was created with absorbent powders and corrective pigments that provide a fresh look and incredible hold while allowing skin to breathe for a full 16 hours. Silica beads capture excess oil to keep the polymer film intact, while the spherical micropowders act like a color stabilizer, ensuring the color does not change or darken during the day. 

What else you need to know:
This foundation has full coverage and a matte finish. 

Research results:
In a self-evaluation of 15 women, this foundation provided 16-hour hold after daily application.
