What it is:
A mineral powder foundation for flawless natural coverage with an ultra-luxurious silky finish.

What it is formulated to do:
Oxygen Mineral Powder contains soybean complex, a unique oxygen-delivering agent that helps promote skin's health, while allowing the skin to breathe. The silky-smooth formula absorbs oil and shine to create a soft, matte finish, while the added vitamin E helps condition and nourish the skin. Oxygen Powder does not contain talc or oil.

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
- Synthetic Fragrances
- Synthetic Dyes
- Phthalates
