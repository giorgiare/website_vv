What it is:
A small, portable foundation stick that's perfect for spot coverage, touch-ups, and travel.

What it does:
Keep your foundation looking fresh all day with Bobbi's 'On-the-Go' Face Touch Up Stick. Carry this mini stick in your makeup bag, and use it to conceal any imperfections, redness, or blemishes. Its creamy, full-coverage formula is perfect for the cheeks, nose, forehead, and chin (not the undereye area), and is ideal for normal and combination skin types.
