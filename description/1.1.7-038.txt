What it is:
A universal concealer, brightener, and illuminator with antiaging and hydrating benefits for the eye area. 

What it is formulated to do:
This multitasking treatment and concealer product is specifically formulated for the eye area to create a naturally more rested-, awakened-, and brightened-looking eye. Featuring mineral-based SPF 35 protection, this concealer protects without irritating, and can be used to camouflage imperfections on targeted areas of the face as well as an eye primer for smooth, long-wearing makeup. This crease-proof and easily blendable concealer comes in one universal shade, is non-drying, and is fully loaded with antiaging agents that deliver results both instantly and over time. 

What else you need to know:
This product conceals discoloration, dark circles, imperfections, and tired looking eyes.
