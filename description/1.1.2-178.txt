What it is: 
An all-in-one color correcting cream to illuminate, brighten, and provide natural-looking coverage and a youthful glow. 

What it is formulated to do: 
CC Glow illuminates and brightens skintone with a ruby crystal complex and stabilized vitamin C. It conceals imperfections for a more even, uniform complexion and provides natural-looking coverage. It also hydrates to smooth away the appearance of lines and wrinkles, and protects against the signs of aging while providing protection against UVA/UVB radiation and damaging free radicals. 

What it is formulated WITHOUT: 
- Parabens 
- Sulfates 
- Phthalates 

What else you need to know:
This product is for all skin types (light to medium skintones). It is formulated with mineral-based sunscreens and is suitable for skin sensitive to chemical filters.
