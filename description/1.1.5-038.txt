What it is: 
An ultrafine mist that sets your makeup all day with a velvety matte finish. 

What it does: 
This unique spray technology delivers micronized powder particles that wrap the complexion in a soft veil and set makeup for sheer coverage that lasts all day. The formula is made with Matt’Smart Complex™, which smooths and perfects skin and absorbs oil, and Nylon-6, a smart fiber with unifying properties to soften irregularities. Kaolin clay also helps to eliminate shine for a perfect matte complexion every time. 

What else you need to know: 
This product comes in one universal shade suitable for all skintones.
