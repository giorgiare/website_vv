What it is:
A featherweight foundation with light to medium coverage and SPF 30 protection. 

What it does:
Weightless and skin-nourishing, this foundation is clinically proven to increase hydration and brighten and smooth skin while softening the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. Formulated with vitamin C, tamarind seed, and marine algae extracts, its versatile coverage lasts for up to eight hours. Water encapsulation technology enables a refreshingly soft finish.
