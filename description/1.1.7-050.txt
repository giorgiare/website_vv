What it is:
A lightweight liquid concealer that helps reduce the appearance of skin imperfections. 

What it does:
This concealer has a polished finish and a formulation that offers optimal coverage for reducing the appearance of blemishes, pigmentation spots, and dark circles. With a unique blend of ingredients such as Karité oil and rice wax, Ellis Faas concealer instantly blends into the skin for a flawless look. The formula also features vitamins E and C, which hydrate and provide antiaging and regenerating properties. 

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Synthetic Fragrances
- Triclosan
