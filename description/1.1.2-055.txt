What it is:
A liquid foundation with UVA/UVB SPF 15 protection that provides luminous, long-lasting coverage. 

What it does:
This luxurious foundation helps counter the signs of aging with cutting-edge ingredients. It also contains Skin Tightener ST™ PH to improve the skin's texture and promote a younger-looking complexion. 

What it is formulated WITHOUT:

- Phthalates 

What else you need to know:
Veil Fluid Makeup is vegan and formulated without oil. 

Research results:
Twice daily application of Matrixyl Synthe'6 over a two-month period was shown to have a lifting effect on forehead lines and wrinkles around the eyes. The wrinkle filler, derived from a tri-peptide found in collagen, treats wrinkles from the inside by rebuilding the skin.
