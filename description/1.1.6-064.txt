What it is:
A matte, translucent, oil-absorbing powder.

What it does:
This powder, suitable for all skintones, protects skin and absorbs shine while setting makeup to create a flawless finish, even under the brightest of movie set lights. The velvety-smooth, silica-based powder blurs fine lines for a soft and even look.

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens

What else you need to know:
Can be worn alone or paired with foundation or primer.
