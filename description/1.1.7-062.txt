What it is:
A brightening concealer to lift, illuminate, and create flawless coverage. 

What it is formulated to do:
Infused with warm peach tones to flatter every skintone, long-wearing Skin Base Lift blends easily, correcting uneven pigmentation and visibly lifting the eye area. It is designed to work in conjunction with Hydra Veil to refresh and rehydrate the area under the eye which is prone to unflattering and unwanted cool undertones and fatigue. For added brightening effect, custom mix each shade with Skin Base Lift White Light. 

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates 
- Phthalates
