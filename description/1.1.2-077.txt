What it is:
A lightweight, oil-free foundation that gives skin the ultimate coverage, comfort, and wear for a flawless matte complexion. 

What it does:
The innovative oil-free formula glides on evenly to visibly minimize the appearance of pores. Skin is left with a soft, smooth look thanks to oil-absorbing powders. The longwearing formula feels lightweight and comfortable on skin and lasts up to eight hours. It provides medium-to-full, buildable coverage and a soft, natural, matte finish.
