What it is:
A loose complexion powder that provides a whisper-weight, natural finish.

What it does:
Talc free, this silky formula is air-milled and blended with spherical soft-focus particles, reducing excess shine and the appearance of imperfections, without settling into lines. It achieves the look of matte, pore-less, polished skin.

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
