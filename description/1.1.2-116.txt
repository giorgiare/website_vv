What it is:
A cream foundation that provides rich, luxurious moisture for the complexion and can be housed in a handy compact.

What it does:
This creamy, moisturizing foundation provides medium-to-full coverage while exclusive prismatic technology creates a dewy and lustrous finish that lasts all-day long. It glides on easily for a perfectly even application. Suitable for all skin types, day by day skin texture gets more refined for a healthy, glowing complexion.
