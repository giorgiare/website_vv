What it is:
An all-in-one antiaging treatment, sunscreen, complexion corrector, moisturizer, and tint. 

What it is formulated to do:
This multifunctional complexion corrector is formulated with arbutin and camu camu, an Amazonian superfruit that has 30 times the vitamin C of an average orange. Vitamin C evens out the skintone and supports natural collagen, creating the appearance of firmer, brighter, younger-looking skin over time. It instantly minimizes the look of imperfections, while broad spectrum SPF 30 protects skin from the sun’s harmful rays. Antioxidant vitamins C and E help fight free radical attack and keep skin healthy, radiant, and beautiful. 

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Phthalates
