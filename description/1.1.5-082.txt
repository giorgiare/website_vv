What it is:
A combination moisturizer/primer. 

What it does:
This lightweight lotion soothes and moisturizes, creating a smooth surface for makeup to adhere to—an essential first step before applying airbrush makeup. Chamomile extract soothes sensitive skin, while cornflower extract helps to keep moisture levels in check. Excellent as a combination moisturizer/primer for oil-rich skintypes, it is also a phenomenal eye base for OCC’s Loose Colours, offering incredible longevity. 

What else you need to know:
This product is a must under OCC Skin™ for a smoother, more even finish.
