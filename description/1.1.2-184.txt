What it is:
A hybrid foundation that imparts a healthy glow. 

What it does:
This hybrid foundation perfects the skin with the sheer coverage of a powder. The Terracotta Skin texture is extremely creamy thanks to a blend of powders and volatile oils combined with an elastomer gel. In just a few seconds, its texture melts into the skin to reveal a healthy-looking complexion. 

What else you need to know:
This product is available in three shades.
