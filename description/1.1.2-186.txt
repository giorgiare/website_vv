What it is:
A six-in-one tinted makeup that neutralizes, primes, moisturizes, brightens, soothes, and helps skin look healthy and youthful. 

What it does:
This cream is specially tinted to color-correct redness, discoloration, and imperfections. It also contains red seaweed that naturally primes, hyaluronic acid that moisturizes and helps diminish the appearance of fine lines, and vitamin C that helps brighten. Additionally, aloe, chamomile, calendula, and white peony help soothe, while antiaging antioxidants such as vitamins A and E and green and white tea help skin look healthy and youthful. 

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates 
- Phthalates 

What else you need to know:
This product is free of oil, silicone, and fragrance and is suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.
