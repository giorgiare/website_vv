What it is:
An extremely light liquid foundation that melts into the skin. 

What it does:
Ellis Faas believes that foundation should illuminate the skin without feeling or appearing like a mask. With an exceptional balance of high-quality ingredients, Skin Veil glides on in a thin, natural-looking layer, blending into the skin. The formula features minerals that diffuse light across the skin’s surface, reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. It also contains moisturizing ingredients that nourish and SPF 15 PA+++ for ultimate sun protection. 

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Synthetic Fragrances
-Triclosan 

What else you need to know:
This foundation is free of perfume and is suitable for all skin types including sensitive.
