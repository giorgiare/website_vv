What it is:
A 100% oil-free face primer formulated for sensitive or oily skin. 

What it does:
This colorless, oil-free formula mattifies surface oil and minimizes redness from irritated skin while it erases the appearance of pores, fine lines, and other imperfections. It keeps foundations and powders in place all day, so that colors stay true, and skin has a smooth and poreless finish. 

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
- Synthetic Fragrances
- Synthetic Dyes
- Petrochemicals
- Phthalates
- GMOs
- Triclosan
