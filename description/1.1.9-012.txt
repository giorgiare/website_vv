What it is:
An ultrasoft and smooth pressed bronzing powder. 

What it does:
This formula was created using a fresh combination of translucent powders and micropearls that deliver a naturally radiant, buildable color. In versatile pink shades, these powders add a touch of soft pink and a flattering lifting effect. 

What else you need to know:
This pair of limited edition compacts feature inspirational phrases,“Be Pretty” and “Be Bold,” embossed into the powder. Antigua is ideal for fair to light skintones; Pink Peony is perfect for medium to deep skintones.
