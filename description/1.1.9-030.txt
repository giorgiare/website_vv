What it is:
A creamy-smooth bronzer that infuses skin with a natural-looking, sun-kissed glow and a subtle island scent. 

What it does:
Decide just how hot you want to be. This bronzer imparts 100% buildable coverage that’s built for your skintone and imparts a subtle, luminous finish. Along with rice lipids for creamy texture and smooth application, it also contains a coconut oil skin emollient that gives off a lightly toasted coconut scent. Adding to the fun, a custom-embossed design evokes sand and waves.
