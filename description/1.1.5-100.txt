What it is:
A primer that creates a stunning, dewy effect.

What it does:
It's important that an artist always prepares his or her canvas. Use this primer under your foundation or on its own for a smooth, even base that is protected from the elements. Containing UVA and UVB filters, Illamasqua Satin Primer lifts and highlights the face, creating a stunning dewy effect to enhance the siren within.
