What it is:
A translucent, colorless setting powder.

What it does:
AmazingCosmetics PowderSet is formulated to work with Velvet Mineral Powder Foundation and the AmazingConcealer as the final part of the signature three simple steps to a flawless face.

PowderSet is formulated to set your makeup and give your skin the perfect finishing touch. PowderSet can also be applied after the AmazingConcealer to give "step one" an extra boost. PowderSet is a favorite of the pros, as the colorless setting powder is such a versatile and convenient beauty tool. Just pop it in your purse to use throughout the day to refresh your look.
