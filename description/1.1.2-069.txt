Nars Powder Foundation introduces a lightweight formula that provides flexible coverage and protection against harmful UVA and UVB rays. Enclosed in a luxury compact, Powder Foundation combines the ease of a powder with the performance of a foundation for a polished complexion. Incredibly versatile, Powder Foundation may be applied with a powder brush for a sheer and natural finish; applied with the sponge applicator for a polished and smooth finish; or applied over Nars liquid foundation for a high-performance, flawless finish.

Unique to Nars Powder Foundation are the inclusion of active ingredients that offer dual protection against UVA and UVB rays. With an SPF 12, Powder Foundation offers protection against UVB rays, and the addition of PA++ indicates optimal protection against UVA rays that may lead to premature signs of aging, dark spots, and wrinkles.
