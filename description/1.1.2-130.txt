What it is:
A dual-finish compact for adjustable coverage and radiance in a powder texture. 

What it does:
Achieve a perfectly even and natural complexion with a satin finish. Whether used as a finishing powder or a foundation, mother-of-pearl micro-particles and iridescent powders enhance the skin’s natural radiance. Enclosed in the compact are a sponge for a natural foundation result and a brush for a light powder fixing finish. 

What else you need to know:
It is available in a range of 10 shades to match any skintone with sheer coverage and a natural finish.
